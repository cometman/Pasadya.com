Danielle Selby is the designer and artist behind Pasadya. As a recent graduate of Texas Tech University with an undergraduate and graduate degree in interior design, Danielle gravitates toward functional designs with simplicity and beauty in mind, every once in a while mixed with humor.

She is in love with painting, transforming old, drab furniture into loved household items, designing heartfelt cards to spread joy, and branding business images, to name a few.

Pasadya was created as an outlet for Daneille to share her love for design-related products and practices. To keep things fresh, products at Pasadya are updated often, so make sure to check in to see what's new!
